Welcome to Mini Reloader Extension!

Thank you for installing the Mini Reloader Extension for Chrome! This extension allows you to easily reload any tab at specific intervals of your choice. Whether you're monitoring websites, auto-refreshing content, or keeping an eye on live updates, Mini Reloader provides a simple and efficient way to automate the process.

Features

  • Custom Time Intervals: Set reload timers with precision using hours, minutes, and seconds input.
  • Presets: Quickly choose from common reload intervals with just one click.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark themes for a comfortable browsing experience.
  • Automatic Tab Refresh: Automatically refresh any tab without manual intervention after the set time.
  • Real-time Timer Display: View the remaining time before the next reload directly in the popup.

With the Mini Reloader Extension, you can effortlessly stay on top of live updates or reload websites without constantly pressing the refresh button. It's perfect for users who need continuous updates from their favorite sites.
